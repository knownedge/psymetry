PSYMETRY™ is a video game developed by an independent artist instead of a corporation. This means that it is a labor of love from a fan of old-school classic FPS action games made specifically for fellow fans of classic FPS games. PSYMETRY™ is going to be a fast action, brutal and trippy First-Person Shooter game with maze levels and tons of secret areas, weapons and more.

The fact that this game project is being made by an independent artist instead of a corporation has advantages and disadvantages. It means that it is a work of passion and will be free of the corrupt shenanigans of corporate suits that are plaguing our industry right now so the focus will be on serving the players, instead of shareholders. On the other hand, It also means that it does not have access to the virtually unlimited resources these corporations have and thus can not hire much staff without support from the gaming community. I am doing my best but there is only so much I can do on my own which is why I am asking for your support.

With your help, I will strive to bring back the glory of classic FPS games to the modern era. The more support I get the better able I will be to add and implement quality content and features such as improved weapons, monsters and animations. If fundraising goes well and many of you back the project, even deathmatch and coop multiplayer support could be added which isn't in the cards right now. Your contributions will have a direct impact on the quality of the final game. Making a high quality game like this is expensive and requires a lot of labor and time. With your support I will be able to contract out tasks and speed up development so we can all play it together sooner.

With Gratitude,

Dan
